mai lakh kh dun ki akash hun jami hun mai,
magar use to khabar hai kimkuchh nhi hun mai,
ajib log hain meri talash me mujh ko,
vha pe dhoondh rhe hai jha nhi hun  mai,
mai aaino se to mayush laut aya tha,
magar kisi ne btaya bhu hasin hun mai
sitaron aao meri rah me bikhar jao,
ye mera hukm hai halanki kuchh nhi hun mai.
